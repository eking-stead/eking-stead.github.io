Deliverable Details & Checklists

This is your project command center. Instead of hunting through the timeline, you can see all the specific tasks required for each of the five final deliverables. Click any item to see a consolidated checklist of what you need to do to complete it.

Task Checklist:

  • [Day 3] Complete basic 3D modeling tutorials.
  • [Day 3] Build a simple version of your object in the software.
  • [Day 4] Build your first complete digital prototype ("Version 1") and submit the `.stl` file for a test print.
  • [Day 6] Review your "Version 1" physical print for flaws and design improvements.
  • [Day 6] Begin iterating on your digital model to create "Version 2".
  • [Day 8] Make final design tweaks based on cost and function.
  • [Day 8] Submit your "Final Version" `.stl` file for the production print.
  • [Day 11] Do any post-processing on your final print (e.g., removing supports).

Task Checklist:

  • [Day 2] Perform simple market research on your product idea.
  • [Day 2] Create a detailed, labeled **Object Sketch** showing multiple angles (top, side, front) and approximate dimensions.

Example Cost vs. Price Breakdown:

Your business plan needs to show you've calculated your costs and set a reasonable price. This chart shows an example based on the Day 5 & 7 tasks.

Task Checklist:

  • [Day 5] Use the slicer software to find your object's material usage (in grams) and print time.
  • [Day 5] Begin your **Business Plan** document and complete **Part 1: Cost Analysis** (Filament Cost + Printer Time = Total Cost).
  • [Day 7] Complete **Part 2: Target Market** (Who is your customer?).
  • [Day 7] Complete **Part 3: Pricing Strategy** (Determine and justify your selling price).

Task Checklist:

  • [Day 9] Begin designing your **Advertisement Poster** (e.g., using Canva, Google Slides).
  • [Day 10] Finalize and complete your poster.

Task Checklist:

  • [Day 10] Begin your final **Project Write-up** by answering the four reflection questions.
  • [Day 11] Review and finalize your write-up.
