Project Overview

Your Mission

Your goal is to design, print, and market a unique 3D printed product. You will take your idea from a simple sketch to a finished product and present it to your peers on "Market Day."

The project starts **today, November 3rd,** and concludes with final presentations on **Friday, November 21st**.

The 5 Key Deliverables

  • 1. 3D Printed Object
  • 2. Object Sketch
  • 3. Business Plan
  • 4. Advertisement Poster
  • 5. Project Write-up

Full 8-Session Timeline

  • Day 1 (Mon, 11/3): Ideation & Sketch

    Intro, brainstorming, and market research. Deliverable: Object Sketch

  • Day 2 (Wed, 11/5): 3D Modeling

    Introduction to 3D modeling software and V1 work time.

  • Day 3 (Fri, 11/7): Submit V1 Model

    Complete and submit your "Version 1" digital file for test printing.

  • Day 4 (Tues, 11/11): Prototype & Business

    Review V1 test prints, iterate on your model, and start your Deliverable: Business Plan.

  • Day 5 (Thurs, 11/13): Final Model & Marketing

    Submit your Final 3D Model and begin your Deliverable: Ad Poster.

  • Day 6 (Mon, 11/17): Final Materials

    Complete your Ad Poster and Deliverable: Project Write-up.

  • Day 7 (Wed, 11/19): "Market Day" Prep

    Finalize your 3D Object (finishing/assembly) and practice your 2-minute pitch.

  • Day 8 (Fri, 11/21): "Market Day" Presentations!

    Set up your "booth," present your final product, and review your peers' work.
